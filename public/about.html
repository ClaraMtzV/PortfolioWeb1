About Me

I am a proud Mexican Immigrant working towards freelancing so I can spend time with my son while I make money. I have been learing programming since I was 15 years old. I grew up not knowing what computers were and what they did, until I took a course in High School in Mexico. That's when I decided that I wanted to be a software developer. I took more programming courses in the US, and I went straight to finishing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a 3.45 gpa.

I remember my first programming assignment was writing down coordinates to draw the alphabet on a turtle app. I loved the fact that I could create something that didn't exist by just typing. Eventhough I have been through rough times while programming, I doubt there's something out there that gives me more satisfaction than seeing my program compile and run.

I also love cooking and baking. I wanted to be a chef before I discover the power of computers. I love watching cooking shows because I learn a lot! I would say that I could eat anything thrown at me ONLY if it doesn't have crazy texture, and it doesn't look scary.
